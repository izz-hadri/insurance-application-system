The healthcare and medical landscape is ever changing. The advancement of science and medicine has certainly paved to countless new treatment and healthcare services. With the ever increasing demand for better healthcare, medical practitioners are also exposed to medical negligence and therefore faced with the challenges of protecting themselves against this exposure.

Medical negligence or publicly known as medical malpractice, refers to failure to deliver the proper standard of care in the treatment of a patient, by accident or oversight or incompetence or a combination of two or more of these elements, resulting in death or injury or disability to the patient.

Defending those claims are not only time consuming, costly and stressful, it can also taint the reputation of medal practitioners.
